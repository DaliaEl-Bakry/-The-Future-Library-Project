why choose us?

From stunning architectural design, extensive books collections, and wide range of services. Future Library offers an unparalleled experience for visitors.

  • Design: Future Library is known for its magnificent structure that's both visually stunning and functional.
  • Collections: The library boasts a collection of more than 1.5 million items, including books, audio and video recordings, and digital resources.
  • Technology: Future Library is equipped with the latest technology to serve its users, such as free Wi-Fi, computers, and online databases.
  • Services: The library offers a wide range of services for its users, such as free workshops, classes, and events.
  • Accessibility: Future Library is fully accessible to people with disabilities, with features such as wheelchair access, and special collections for people with visual impairments.
